Humidity





Humidity is fairly regular across latitudes. However, tropical climates (between -23.5 and 23.5 latitude) tend to have the highest humidity year-round. Arctic (66.5 latitude) and Antartic (-66.5) will have the lowest humidity year-round. Everything in between varies somewhat with the season.
